About SwachhUni

SwachhUni is a campus cleanliness initiative that empowers students, teachers, and staff to report hygiene and maintenance issues across university campuses. Our platform promotes cleanliness, accountability, and sustainability through community participation.

By providing an easy way to report issues and track their resolution, SwachhUni helps create a cleaner, healthier, and more pleasant campus environment for everyone.

Reporting issues through SwachhUni helps:

  • Maintain a clean and hygienic campus environment
  • Prevent small issues from becoming major problems
  • Create a sense of community ownership and responsibility
  • Provide valuable data to university administration for better resource allocation
  • Ensure a pleasant and professional learning environment

Our process is simple and efficient:

  1. Spot an Issue: Notice an area that needs attention
  2. Report: Use our form to submit details and photos
  3. Review: Our team validates and prioritizes the issue
  4. Resolution: University maintenance addresses the problem
  5. Confirmation: You receive updates and confirmation when resolved

You can report various campus issues including:

Cleanliness Issues

  • Dirty restrooms
  • Overflowing trash bins
  • Unclean common areas
  • Spills and stains

Maintenance Issues

  • Broken furniture
  • Faulty lighting
  • Leaking pipes
  • Electrical issues

Infrastructure Issues

  • Damaged pathways
  • Broken windows
  • Parking area problems
  • Landscape issues

We believe in recognizing and rewarding our most active contributors:

  • Certificates: Monthly certificates for top contributors
  • Digital Badges: Earn badges for different achievement levels
  • Leaderboard: Feature on our monthly leaderboard
  • University Recognition: Special recognition from university administration
  • Prize Incentives: occasional prizes for exceptional contributors

Points are awarded based on the severity and impact of reported issues, with bonus points for timely reporting and follow-up.

We take your privacy and data security seriously:

  • Personal information is only used for reporting and communication purposes
  • Report data is anonymized in public displays
  • We comply with all applicable data protection regulations
  • You can request deletion of your data at any time
  • All data is stored securely with encryption

For more details, please refer to our complete Privacy Policy available on the university website.
